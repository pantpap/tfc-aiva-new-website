Join the PEERS Co-Creation Group

You are welcome to join the PEERS Co-Creation Group via the QR or link https://tinyurl.com/peers-request.

PEERS primarily targets Europe's CBRN-E practitioners, European research policymaking as well as other stakeholders including the research community and national standardisation bodies.

We welcome stakeholder engagement so that we work in a manner that best suits our primary targets, giving the approved registered members of the Co-creation Group access to the PEERS platform within the surrounding ecosystem. We operate in a manner that is orientated towards European front line practitioners and policy makers and apply a bottom up approach with a view to strengthen European preparedness and response to a CBRN-E incident.
