TFC Research and Innovation Limited

TFC is a global provider of engaged scientific research services, working across the following pillars:
  • clustering, better practice guides, conceptual architecture, project management, standardization, outreach and proposal development.

Our Approach

Our approach is highly client centric. We get to know and understand client needs and requirements to provide knowledge, know-how capability and plans in support of a proposed undertaking. Through our ingrained approach, TFC applies it in scientific research at national and European levels. The company brings this approach to project work, proposal development, inhouse and external initiatives as well as to process development with lateral thinking and fresh ideas integrated into proceedings. Our approach can be applied at many levels including strategic, policy and operational. Our skills base is used to help with the delivery of better outcomes and results from the above listed work activities.

Partnership

TFC operates in a collaborative manner, supporting partners and clients to advance research and innovation for the improvement of their products, services, initiatives and processes as well as for the support of market, industry and academia acceptance. We partner many organisations working across multiple domains, including eHealth, Operational Security, ICT, Aquaculture and Energy.
