How can AI/smart technology support post cancer treatment patients

We all know a person who has battled cancer. Mental health and depression after treatment can be difficult and their quality of life significantly reduced. So what can technology do about it? Listen to Christine O'Meara and Philip O'Brien from the Walton Institute, Ireland, on what they have to say in answering this question as they speak opening about Artificial Intelligence and Federate Learning and how it can contribute to improving the life of the post cancer treatment patient. TFC work closely with the Walton Institute in the FAITH project and this Podcast was produced in collaboration with Ubitech, Greece, who lead the Podcast series in the Cancer Survivorship - AI for Well Being Cluster.
