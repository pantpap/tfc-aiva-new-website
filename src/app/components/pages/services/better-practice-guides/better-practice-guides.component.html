Better Practice Guides

Thanks to our European research and engagement in standardization, the new Better Practice Guide initiative (BPG) is born. A BPG is an entity in its own right and sits in the pre-nominative standardization space. It is driven by practitioners (i.e., people in the know) and at reasonable cost. Guides are developed in support, available for use and reference, residing in the TFC repository. The development of a Better Practice Guide is applicable to most sectors.
