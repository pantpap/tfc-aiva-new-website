Project management is the process of leading teams and work undertaken to achieve goals and objectives to specific criteria within a defined timeframe. TFC undertake project management and related activities (e.g., risk and quality management) across European and national research projects. The company is active in this capacity across a number of European projects, such as in the FAITH project, which involves AI/Federate Learning in post- cancer treatment stages, which is led by the South East Technological University, Ireland.
