Standardization

The company is actively involved in standardization at national, European and International levels, leading in Ireland the NSAI TC99 standardisation committee and some employees are an active in the European CEN TC391 societal and citizen standardization committee, the CEN Security Sector Forum as well as the ISO 292 Security and resilience committee. In many European projects, TFC leads in the area of standardization activities, such as in the recently completed European projects, NO-FEAR and SPEEDIER, and in ongoing projects, e.g., PEERS and FAITH.
TFC lead and support the development of outreach plans and activities for communications, dissemination and exploitation. Each research project is poised with different challenges and requires fresh thinking to help with the reach to target audiences and market acceptance. The company helps with delivery of outreach services often leading or partnering management teams in this capacity and we develop and refine Goto market strategies in support based on our applied lateral thinking approach.
