Services

TFC leverages its’ skill base to enable research to be realised that contributes to the understanding of our changing world in a social and technological context and contributes to its evolution. Research is targeted at improving the quality of people’s lives, in areas such as Societal and citizen resilience, Cancer-survivorship and AI for well-being, Energy transition and separately, Inclusion at national and European level. Our Services provision includes:

Clustering

We instigate stakeholder engagement through the development of Cluster Groups who have a specific interest on a topic and bring different intelligence to the table so that perceived understanding and perspectives can be ascertained with follow up supporting actions.

Better Practice Guides

Thanks to our European research and engagement in standardization, the new Better Practice Guide initiative (BPG) is born.

Conceptual Architecture

TFC has a skill set that embodies creative thinking. The company utilizes its’ conceptualism approach for much of our work, characterized by the introduction of lateral thinking and fresh ideas to expand an initial or drafted concept, which may/may not be instigated inhouse, by a partner or client.

Project Management

Project management is the process of leading teams and work undertaken to achieve goals and objectives to specific criteria within a defined timeframe. TFC undertake project management and related activities

Standardization

The company is actively involved in standardization at national, European and International levels, leading in Ireland the NSAI TC99 standardisation committee and some employees are an active in the European CEN TC391 societal and citizen standardization committee, the CEN Security Sector Forum as well as the ISO 292 Security and resilience committee.

Proposal Development

Horizon Europe is the EU's key funding programme for Research & Innovation with a budget of €95.5 billion. It will run from 2021 to 2027. TFC is involved in a number of Horizon Europe projects and developed a number of successful proposals.
