  • May 2023

White Paper

Why not read the White Paper produced by the Cluster - Cancer Survivorship - AI for Well-Being, which is a document featuring the Cluster members' recommendation on designing #eHealth solutions? The "Cancer Survivorship - AI for Well-Being" is a group of #EU projects researching #HighTech applications in #healthcare. Specifically, the 11 Cluster members are developing #AI-based, #digital supportive tools for #cancer patients' and survivors' #wellbeing, each solution tackling a different aspect of #QualityofLife. Since its formation by TFC through the Faith project, the Cluster has always valued the importance of #synergy and cooperation among members, in a process of continuous knowledge sharing and comparison between the members' different experiences. The CS_AIW White Paper gathers all the lessons learned by the Cluster members while working on the design of their #eHealth solutions, from technological barriers to end-users' involvement in the designing phase.
